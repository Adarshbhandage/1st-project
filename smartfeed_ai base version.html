The System

A closed-loop intelligence
cycle in 4 steps

From student input to optimized cooking — the system runs automatically, every single day.

01

Student Confirms

Daily notification: Will you attend breakfast, lunch, or dinner? Yes / No / Maybe — takes 5 seconds.

02

AI Predicts

Behavioral model analyzes past patterns to adjust unreliable responses and forecast true attendance.

03

Mess Optimizes

Admin gets exact cooking quantities per dish, adjusted for consumption patterns and waste history.

04

System Learns

Every meal, the model improves. Predictions get sharper. Waste goes down week over week.
